a little background...

DT.renovation is a locally owned and operated business based in Cary, North Carolina, serving the entire Research Triangle area. We specialize in cosmetic reconstruction, socket replacement, painting, bathroom remodeling, floor laying, and general maintenance of houses. Our company is led by the experienced and dedicated professional, Dmytro Tkach, from Odessa, Ukraine, with a passion for creating beautiful and functional living spaces. We're not a big corporation, but we pride ourselves on providing personalized, top-quality work and excellent customer service to our valued clients in the community. Whether you're looking to update a single room or your entire house, our team of skilled professionals will work closely with you to bring your vision to life. Trust us to take care of all your home reconstruction needs in the Research Triangle area.
